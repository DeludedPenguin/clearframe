Draw the Frame

You are not planning. You are noticing.

Focus

What’s worth your energy today? These are your guiding anchors. Not a list—just 1 to 3 things that matter.

Friction

What’s slowing you down? Interruptions, doubts, blockers. Internal or external.

Signal

What keeps showing up—despite your plans? These are the breadcrumbs. Track them.

Drift

Where did you go without meaning to? Distraction? Or maybe a message?

How to begin

Take a blank page or open your preferred app. Draw a 2x2 grid and label the quadrants: Focus, Friction, Signal, Drift.

Fill it out gently. Don’t balance. Just observe.
